About Us

PQAR (Plex Quantum Atomic Research) is dedicated to advancing research in nuclear engineering, particle physics, and theoretical physics. Our projects aim to push the boundaries of scientific knowledge and technological innovation. But, PQAR is not a company at all! We are a project in and of itself, created to make more projects... woah! All of our projects are solely for research, and we do not have any physical nuclear reactors or particle accelerators, but we do have extremely precise simulations and lots of models to help us fulfill our goals.

Our Projects

  • IB-CLS: A custom Linux system for various hardware applications.
  • MMTR-2380: A smaller Magnox Reactor for research and radiation testing.
  • MRPA: A Modular Research Particle Accelerator designed for muon and dark matter research.
  • TPR: A Transmutation Power Reactor for processing used nuclear fuels and generating energy efficiently.
  • PQAR?: Plex Quantum Atomic Research itself!
